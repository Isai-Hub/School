SPORTS

San Francisco consists of three big teams each in different sports, which are the NFL, NBA and MLB. The teams are the San Francisco Giants, Golden State Warriors and 49ers. These teams are known for their history of championships. The Giants consist of 8 World Series championships. The Golden State Warriors have 7 NBA championships and the 49ers have 5 Super Bowl championships. The beautiful atmosphere in these games brings attention to tourists. Take a game visit and enjoy the beautiful games of the Bay Area.
